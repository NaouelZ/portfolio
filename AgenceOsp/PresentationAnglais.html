Health Community+





OSP Agency is launching its own project! A website that suggests adequate solutions to each need a disabled person may express. The website will include a geolocation system to list every places that were thought to make their life easier such as gyms, hospitals or schools. Our goal while making this site was to make those places known to disabled people, to make them feel welcome. We want to better their daily lives by making a platform that will make all those necessary places accessible for disabled people. Moreover, the platform will contain chats and forums about this theme through which people will be able to interact.
