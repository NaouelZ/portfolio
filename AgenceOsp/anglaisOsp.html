OSP Agency

OSP (stands for Healthcare Organisatio) is a young french start-up born in 2017. We are a communication agency specialized in sanitary and social. Our goal comes from the will to make daily life easier to disabled people, aged people and people who suffer from serious diseases. We are solicited by several companies to participate in the promotion of their projects. Therefore, we are enabling a communication network to give more visibility to those companies, associations, other agencies and their projects.
